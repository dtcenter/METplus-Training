METplus Training¶

Developed by the Developmental Testbed Center, Boulder, CO

WARNING! WARNING! WARNING!¶

August 2020: These training modules are under active development and not yet intended for use!

WARNING! WARNING! WARNING!¶

Welcome to the METplus Training modules!

Please see the METplus Website to learn more about this project.

These training materials describe the use of the METplus Software and its components (MET, METviewer, METexpress, METplotpy, METcalcpy, and METdatadb).

Users are encouraged to start by setting up a Installation and Setup so they can follow along with the exercises. Or, if you prefer, you may navigate directly to the training modules and simply watch the videos.
